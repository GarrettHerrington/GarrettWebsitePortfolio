Garrett's Movie List

Here are my top 3 movies of all time

The Thing

The thing is my favorite Sci-Fi horror film of all time. Stunning performance by Kurt Russel, impeccable writing,
and well done conventional special effects make this movie a must see.

Fight Club

A controversial film that offers a lot of thought provoking ideas. Starring Brad Pitt and Edward Norton,
Fight Club is a zeigtgeist that captures the psyche of many information age, middle class men.
The writing is impeccable and Brad Pitt as well as Edward Norton give dedicated performances.

Princess Mononoke

My favorite Miyazaki film. Princess Mononoke is a fairy tale in which fantastical spirits and beasts bid for control of the land.
The themes of the film are thought provoking and the story will leave you wanting more.
